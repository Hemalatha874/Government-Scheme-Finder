About Our Portal

The Government Scheme Eligibility Finder is an online citizen-centric portal that simplifies search and verification of welfare schemes across India.

Citizens often miss out on central and state government welfare benefits due to the lack of consolidated information and complex eligibility criteria. Our platform acts as a unified bridge, resolving details such as age thresholds, income brackets, educational qualification filters, and state jurisdictions to recommend the right scheme for the right citizen.

Our Objectives & Features

Accuracy First

Our portal utilizes exact rules and schemas collected directly from official sources like myScheme.gov.in, data.gov.in, and india.gov.in to verify scheme details.

Secure Profiles

User demographic information, income details, and uploaded verification certificates are secured in database tables with proper encryption and validation controls.

Instant Recommendations

The automated eligibility engine matches parameters against schemes, categorizing them into Eligible, Partially Eligible, or Not Eligible in seconds.
