Application Guidance

Follow our comprehensive walkthrough to apply successfully for welfare schemes online or offline

How to Apply Online:
  1. 1
    Determine Eligibility & Scheme

    Use the Eligibility Checker on our portal. Identify the scheme you qualify for and click View Details to read requirements.

  2. 2
    Prepare Required Documents

    Scan and prepare PDF/JPG files of Aadhaar, Income Certificate, Community Certificate, and Bank Passbook. Keep them ready in our Documents Section.

  3. 3
    Navigate to Official Portal

    Click the Apply Online button on the Scheme Details page to redirect to the official government registration portal (e.g. NSP, PM-KISAN).

  4. 4
    Submit Form & Track

    Fill in the online form, upload required files, submit, and note down your Application Reference Number for tracking purposes.

How to Apply Offline:
  1. 1
    Download & Print Form

    Download the standard application form using the Download Form button on this page. Print it clearly in black or blue ink.

  2. 2
    Fill Details Legibly

    Complete all columns in block letters. Ensure fields like Name, Age, Income, and Bank Account details match your Aadhaar exactly.

  3. 3
    Attach Self-Attested Photocopies

    Attach physical copies of Aadhaar, Community Certificate, and Income Certificate. Sign across the photocopies to self-attest.

  4. 4
    Submit to Helpdesk

    Visit the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC), Block Development Office (BDO), or the relevant department desk to submit the papers. Obtain an acknowledgment slip.

Application Tips
  • Ensure your Mobile Number is linked to your Aadhaar Card to receive OTPs during verification.
  • Your Bank Account must be linked to Aadhaar (Aadhaar Seeded) to receive Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) funds.
  • Ensure your family Income Certificate is updated (valid for 6 months or 1 year depending on state rules).
  • Never pay fees to anyone. Government scheme registrations are 100% free of cost on official portals.
Typical Processing Times
Scholarships & Education 30 - 45 Days
Agricultural Cash Benefits 15 - 30 Days
Pension Schemes 45 - 60 Days
Housing Subsidies 60 - 90 Days
